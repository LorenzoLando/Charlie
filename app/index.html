About

A PIECE ABOUT MYSELF!

“Born in Aberdeen when attending school took a great interest in art. Became a self- taught artist once I moved to Dundee, there I carried out various commissions including portraiture, street scenes and murals. It was around 1990 that I decided to form my stained glass company Gothic Glass, and for the next 10 years carried out work including designing building and installing stained glass windows and panels for clients . I also carried out restoration work for churches, castles, public buildings and private houses. I then decided to start stained glass classes, which proved to be very successful. I moved to Glasgow for a year to a large stained glass studio, where I worked alongside other artists, learning all forms of glass work. My intention now that I have returned to Aberdeen is to start offering the stained glass classes, and carry out consultation work once again. I also will return to offering to carry out commissions in all aspects of art. Charlie Wood.”

Course 1

The Copper Foil Technique

“Learning how to cut and shape glass, using the various methods. Using copper foil and how to use a soldering iron, creating a practice piece over the time of the course. This method is used to create light catchers, ornaments , eg. jewellery boxes, and Tiffany lampshades, which the method has came to be known as. The course lasts for 10 weeks , with each session lasting 2 hours. ”

Course 2

The Stained Glass Technique

“Learning how to cut and shape glass, using the various methods. Being able to use lead came to construct a stained glass designed piece over the time of the course. This method is used for creating large scale windows for Churches, Cathedrals, Castles , Palaces ,public buildings and private commissions. The course lasts for 10 weeks , with each session lasting 2 hours.”
